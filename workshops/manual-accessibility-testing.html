Manual Accessibility Testing

Format: 1–2 hours, half day, or deep dive
Who it’s for: Individuals or teams, from beginner to advanced

What it covers

Learn how to start testing digital content using real assistive technologies: screen readers, keyboard-only navigation, magnifiers, switch device, and voice control. Sessions are tailored to your needs, tools, and confidence level.

Benefits

  • Builds empathy for diverse interaction methods by highlighting common alternatives to standard inputs.
  • Demonstrates the importance of robust, flexible design to support real-world assistive technology use
  • Helps teams identify barriers early in the design or development process
  • Gain confidence using a range of devices and accessibility tools
