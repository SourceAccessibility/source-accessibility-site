Accessibility Fundamentals

Format: Half day or full day
Who it’s for: Anyone new to accessibility or looking to align teams around inclusive design

What it covers

A comprehensive introduction to digital accessibility and disability awareness. Challenges how teams think about access and inclusion — going beyond compliance to focus on people. Combines key principles with real-life examples and hands-on activities.

Benefits

  • Builds confidence discussing disability and inclusive design
  • Encourages teams to think differently about their audiences
  • Establishes understanding across user, employee, and client touch points
  • Equips teams to spot and fix common accessibility issues at all stages of the product life cycle
