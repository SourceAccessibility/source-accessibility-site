Mentoring

Whether you’re an individual specialist or part of a growing team, mentoring can help build accessibility confidence, clarity, and consistency.

Who It’s For

  • Designers and developers new to accessibility
  • Product teams navigating accessibility challenges
  • Internal champions needing support or direction

What’s Included

  • One-to-one or small group mentoring
  • Accessibility Q&A sessions
  • Practical feedback and confidence-building

Free Support for Newcomers

I offer free 30-minute mentoring sessions to students and individuals new to accessibility, design, or tech. If you’re just getting started, this is a no-pressure space to ask questions and build confidence.

Mentoring can be a one-off or part of ongoing support. Book a free 30-min call to explore what might work for you.
